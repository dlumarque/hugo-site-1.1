LIVE: Soliciting Blog Names!!

At one point while brainstorming what to call this place I found myself in a toss up between “I’m a Developer Baby” and “I’m a Baby Developer”, and realized right away that NEITHER would do well in communicating clearly in my desired connotation what I was trying to say. The first option makes me sound both like I’m the product of a seasoned developer (or pair of developers), and thus naturally am a developer myself, and/or that I’ve undergone some specialized training to be where I am, à-la-“Designer Baby”. What even is a designer baby, anyway? [update: a quick google read reminded me that it’s the very real, if overhyped, term for babies who have been genetically altered in some way for perceived benefit – ie. modifying a gene or set of genes in order to create HIV resistance. This is actually a wild and cool concept that is understandably pretty stigmatized, but discussing this further will digress too far from the original purpose of this article. ahem.]
